The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet of the ground. Each yurt is fully enclose with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove
Your luxury campaing experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge
What should I bring?
Most guests pack confortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing
weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
