Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trail Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayacks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching a Pacific Trails, we offered guided birdwatching trips several times a week.
